Modern Medieval

Allison Bakalars

The story behind my Mapbox map.

I was inspirated by medieval hand-drawn maps and enjoyed the simple, watercolor style. I also liked the muted and natural color palette which I drew upon in my map.

I liked the parchment texture and tried to replicate that in my map. I also added hand-drawn styles of trees to imitate hand-drawn forestry maps. The ocean was one of the most important patterns to me as I felt that the wave pattern is crucial to the medieval style. I also chose to keeep the highway shields as I found their different symbols similar to family crests.

Something I really liked about my map was that I used castles to symbolize cities.

Another thing I found fun to design was swords to symbolize POIs.
